FAQs

What are your operating hours?

-

Our hospital operates 24/7 for emergency services. Outpatient clinics are open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. For specific department hours, please contact us.

What should I bring to my appointment?

+

Please bring your identification card, insurance information, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results.

What specialized services do you offer?

+

We offer specialized services including Oncology, Orthopedics, Dermatology, Psychiatry, and advanced diagnostic imaging.

Do you accept my insurance?

+

We work with a wide range of insurance providers. Please contact our billing department with your insurance details to confirm coverage prior to your visit.
